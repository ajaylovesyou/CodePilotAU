About CodePilot

A next-generation AI-powered music and sound therapy platform built to make music creation and mental wellness accessible for everyone.

🎯 Our Mission

CodePilot was born from a simple idea: everyone has music inside them. We built an AI studio that listens to your hum and transforms it into a full melody — combined with sound therapy so you can create music and relieve stress in the same place. No musical training needed. Just your voice.

🎵 What is CodePilot?

An AI-powered music generation studio that detects the notes you hum, uses Claude AI to compose a personalized melody, and plays it back through 5 instrument modes plus 3 real sound therapy modes — all synthesized in real-time in your browser with no downloads needed.

🧠 The Technology

Built with Web Audio API for real-time pitch detection using autocorrelation algorithms, Tone.js for professional-grade synthesis with reverb, chorus and EQ effects, and the Claude AI API (via a secure backend proxy) for intelligent melody generation that mirrors your unique vocal pattern.

🌟 Key Features
🎙️

Voice-to-Melody

Hum any tune and AI detects your notes in real time and composes a full melody that mirrors your musical intent.

🧘

Sound Therapy

Three clinically-inspired modes — Tibetan Bowls, Binaural Beats, and Sound Bath — for genuine relaxation.

🎹

Interactive Piano

Fully interactive 3-octave piano keyboard with professional synthesis and touch support.

📚

Melody Library

Every melody you generate is automatically saved. Play, re-download, or delete anytime.

⬇️

Export Audio

Download your melodies as WAV or MP3 audio files, rendered with full reverb and mastering effects.

🍪

Remembers You

Your name, theme, and speed preferences are saved in cookies — everything ready when you return.
